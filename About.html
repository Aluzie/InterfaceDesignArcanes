Our Story

The Arcanes Journey

Founded in 2018 by visionary designer Aisya Maisarah Binti Zamahari, Arcanes began as a small workshop in Kuching, dedicated to celebrating Malaysian heritage through contemporary jewelry.

What started as a creative passion project in a modest studio has blossomed into a cherished jewelry brand known for blending traditional Sarawakian motifs with modern aesthetics. Each Arcanes piece embodies Aisya's commitment to quality craftsmanship and cultural storytelling.
