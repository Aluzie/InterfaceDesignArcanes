Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jewelry and services

Yes, we specialize in creating custom jewelry pieces tailored to your preferences. Whether you have a specific design in mind or need guidance, our designers will work closely with you to create a unique piece that reflects your style. Contact us to schedule a consultation.

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase for items in their original condition with proof of purchase. Custom-made pieces cannot be returned unless there's a manufacturing defect. Please contact us before returning any items.

To maintain the beauty of your jewelry, store pieces separately to avoid scratches, remove before swimming or bathing, clean gently with a soft cloth, and have professional cleaning done annually. Different materials require specific care, so check the care instructions provided with your purchase.

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days), express shipping (1-2 business days), and international shipping (7-14 business days). All orders are insured and require signature upon delivery. Free shipping is available for orders over RM500 within Malaysia.

Yes, we provide professional jewelry repair services including chain soldering, stone replacement, ring resizing, clasp replacement, and polishing. We offer free estimates for all repairs. Pieces purchased from Arcanes have special warranty considerations.

We are committed to ethical practices and sustainability. All our gemstones and precious metals are sourced from suppliers who adhere to responsible mining practices. We use recycled metals when possible and ensure our diamonds are conflict-free and Kimberley Process certified.
